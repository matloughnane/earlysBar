Bumper Balls

Bubble Football is the newest craze sweeping the nation! A fun, invigorating and tiring sport- it involves the wearing of bubble balls in an attempt to play football and many other mini games.

Arranmore Bumper Balls is our new venture in the hopes of bringing more people to Arranmore Island. We cater for stag parties, hen parties, corporate events, social clubs, fundraisers etc. A minimum of 10 people is required to run a one hour session and all of the instructors are trained in the safety aspects of the bubble balls, how to safely put them on, take them off and how to conduct yourself on the playing field.
