Directions to Arranmore Island

There are regular ferry services to the island from Burtonport starting early in the morning and running almost hourly throughout the day. Timetables can be found at the harbour in Burtonport, or you can contact the ferry operators directly for more information.

Airports

The closest airports to Arranmore Island and Burtonport are Donegal Airport and City of Derry Airport. Both airports have connecting flights to Dublin.

Donegal Airport www.donegalairport.ie

City of Derry Airport www.cityofderryairport.com

Sligo Airport www.sligoairport.com

Dublin Airport www.dublinairport.com

Belfast City Airport www.belfastcityairport.com

Belfast International Airport www.bial.co.uk
